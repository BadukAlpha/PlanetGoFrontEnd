How the Season Works

Each Go season is structured like a professional league. All registered players are divided into pools, where they compete in a round-robin format. After pool play, the top 64 participants with the best records advance to a single elimination bracket. The winner of the bracket is crowned the season champion.

  • Pool Play: Players are split into pools and play against every other player in their pool.
  • Advancement: The top 64 players overall move on to the next stage.
  • Single Elimination: The final 64 compete in a knockout bracket until only one champion remains.
